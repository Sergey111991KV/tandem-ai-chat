Install

Four steps to your first AI reply

1

Download & open

Open the .dmg and drag Tandem AI Chat into Applications.

2

First launch

macOS will show a Gatekeeper prompt once — the app is notarized by Apple, so it's safe to open.

3

Sign in to Telegram

Connect the same Telegram account you use on iPhone, then pick the chats to monitor.

4

Activate your subscription

Subscribe at checkout, then paste the license key from your email in Settings → Subscription.

4

Optional: support the project

Enjoying Tandem? Leave a donation from Settings → Support — it never unlocks features.
